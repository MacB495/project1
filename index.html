Space Grotesk is a proportional sans-serif typeface variant based on Colophon Foundry's
fixed-width Space Mono family (2016). Originally designed by Florian Karsten in 2018, Space Grotesk retains
the monospace's idiosyncratic details while optimizing for improved readability at non-display sizes.

The lore of the Alien franchise centers on the deadly Xenomorph species and the complex interplay between humanity, corporate greed, and existential questions about creation. The lifecycle of
the Xenomorph begins with eggs laid by a queen, containing facehuggers that implant embryos into living hosts. Once gestated, the embryos burst from the host's chest, leading to the
emergence of a fully grown Xenomorph. This terrifying lifecycle showcases the species' adaptability and lethal efficiency, making them formidable adversaries throughout the series.

These films delve into themes of creation,
responsibility, and the consequences of scientific
experimentation, raising questions about humanitys
place in the universe. Together, the films create a rich
tapestry of horror and philosophical inquiry, exploring
the dark side of human ambition and the existential
dread of encountering the unknown.
